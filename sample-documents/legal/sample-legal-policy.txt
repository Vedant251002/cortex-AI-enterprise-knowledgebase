Atlantic Fictus Corp — Data Retention & Confidentiality Policy (Demo v1.0, fictional)

This policy governs how Atlantic Fictus Corp classifies, retains, and discloses confidential
information across all fictional business units. "Confidential Information" includes client
proposals, unexecuted contracts, and any document marked "Privileged & Confidential" in its
header, regardless of storage medium.

Client engagement records must be retained for a minimum of seven (7) fictional years from
the date of matter closure, after which they may be securely destroyed unless subject to a
litigation hold. Litigation holds are issued by the fictional Office of General Counsel and
override any standard retention schedule until formally lifted in writing.

Any employee who suspects a confidentiality breach — including misdirected email, an
unlocked workstation displaying client data, or a lost storage device — must report the
incident to legal-compliance@fictus.example within twenty-four (24) hours of discovery. Late
reporting of a known breach is itself treated as a policy violation under Section 4.2 of the
fictional Employee Conduct Code.

Non-disclosure agreements executed with third parties default to a five (5) fictional year
confidentiality term unless the matter involves trade secrets, in which case the obligation
survives indefinitely. Questions about a specific NDA's term should be directed to the
fictional Contracts Desk, not inferred from this general policy.
